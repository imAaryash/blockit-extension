# Privacy Policy - BlockIt Focus Extension

**Last Updated**: December 9, 2025

## Overview

BlockIt ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### Data We Collect

1. **Account Information**
   - Username (chosen by you)
   - Display name (chosen by you)
   - Password (hashed with bcrypt - we never store plain text)
   - Avatar emoji (chosen by you)

2. **Usage Statistics**
   - Total focus time
   - Number of sessions completed
   - Sites blocked count
   - Focus streaks (current and longest)

3. **Activity Data**
   - Online/offline status
   - Current activity (focusing, browsing, watching YouTube)
   - YouTube video titles (when watching, for friends to see)
   - Website URLs (only when blocked)

4. **Social Data**
   - Friends list
   - Friend requests (pending and sent)
   - Community chat messages

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Browsing history (except blocked site counts)
- ❌ Personal information (email, phone, address)
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ Cookies or tracking data
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Device fingerprinting

## How We Use Your Data

### Primary Uses

1. **Account Management**
   - Authenticate your login sessions
   - Store your preferences and settings

2. **Statistics Tracking**
   - Display your productivity stats
   - Calculate leaderboard rankings
   - Track focus streaks

3. **Social Features**
   - Connect you with friends
   - Show online status to friends
   - Enable real-time activity sharing
   - Power community chat

### Data Sharing

- **Friends**: Your friends can see:
  - Your display name and avatar
  - Your online status
  - Your current activity (focusing, YouTube, etc.)
  - Your productivity stats (focus time, sessions)
  
- **Leaderboard**: All users can see:
  - Your display name
  - Your total focus time (rank)
  - Your avatar

- **Community Chat**: All users can see:
  - Your messages
  - Your display name
  - Your online status

### Third-Party Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for marketing purposes
- Provide data to analytics companies

## Data Storage

- **Database**: MongoDB Atlas (cloud database)
- **Location**: Cloud servers (MongoDB Atlas data centers)
- **Encryption**: 
  - Passwords: bcrypt hashed (10 rounds)
  - Transmission: HTTPS/WSS encrypted
  - At rest: MongoDB encryption

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data stored indefinitely while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 24 hours
- **Inactive Accounts**: No automatic deletion (you control your account)

## Your Rights

### You Have the Right To:

1. **Access Your Data**
   - View all your stats in the dashboard
   - See your friend list and activity

2. **Update Your Data**
   - Change display name, avatar
   - Update settings
   - Modify friend list

3. **Delete Your Data**
   - Delete your account anytime
   - Settings → Delete Account
   - All data permanently removed

4. **Export Your Data**
   - Contact us to request data export
   - Receive JSON file with all your data

5. **Control Privacy**
   - Choose who can send friend requests
   - Control what friends see
   - Opt out of leaderboard (by deleting account)

## Security

### Security Measures

- **Password Protection**: bcrypt hashing (industry standard)
- **JWT Authentication**: Secure token-based sessions
- **HTTPS Only**: All data transmitted over encrypted connections
- **Input Validation**: Protection against injection attacks
- **Rate Limiting**: Protection against brute force attacks

### Your Responsibility

- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your account credentials
- Log out on shared computers
- Report security issues immediately

## Cookies & Local Storage

- **LocalStorage**: Used to store auth tokens and preferences locally
- **No Cookies**: We don't use cookies for tracking
- **Session Storage**: Temporary session data only
